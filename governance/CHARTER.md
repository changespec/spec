# ChangeSpec Project Charter

**Version:** 1.0
**Effective:** 2026-04-16
**Steward:** Roboticforce Inc.

---

## Mission

The ChangeSpec project develops and maintains an open, versioned, machine-readable specification for communicating software changes from producers to consumers. The specification is published under the Apache 2.0 license and is governed by an open, documented process.

## Scope

The ChangeSpec project has authority over:

- The ChangeSpec specification document and its versioning.
- The canonical JSON Schema (schema.json) for ChangeSpec events.
- The reference implementations published under the ChangeSpec GitHub organization.
- The conformance test suite and certification program.
- The ChangeSpec Working Groups chartered under this project.
- The trademark policy for "ChangeSpec" and "ChangeSpec Certified".

## Non-goals

The ChangeSpec project does not:

- Operate a hosted platform for producing, aggregating, or consuming change events. That function belongs to commercial implementations, including but not limited to the ChangeSpec hosted service at changespec.com.
- Certify that any specific vendor's change communication practices meet regulatory requirements. ChangeSpec certifies conformance with the specification, not regulatory compliance.
- Represent or advocate for any commercial entity in regulatory proceedings. Regulator engagement is on behalf of the open specification, not any commercial interest.
- Compete with or exclude implementations. Any entity may implement the ChangeSpec format. Governance seats are not sold.

## Stewardship

Roboticforce Inc. is the initial steward organization. The steward:

- Holds the copyright on the specification and the trademark registrations.
- Employs the initial maintainers.
- Funds infrastructure (GitHub organization, domain, documentation hosting) during the bootstrap period.

Stewardship may be transferred to a foundation (for example, the Linux Foundation or a dedicated ChangeSpec Foundation) when the contributor community reaches a scale that warrants independent governance. Stewardship transfer requires a unanimous decision of the then-current maintainer group and formal announcement with at least 90 days' notice.

## Relationship to changespec.com

changespec.com is a commercial hosted platform operated by Roboticforce Inc. It is a separate legal and technical entity from the ChangeSpec project. The commercial platform has no special privileges in the governance process. Changes to the specification that serve the commercial platform but not the broader community will not be accepted.

Roboticforce Inc. discloses its commercial interest in every formal governance document, standards body submission, and regulator-facing communication.

## Amendment

This charter may be amended by a unanimous decision of the then-current maintainer group, with at least 30 days' notice and a public comment period. Material changes require the same process as major specification changes.
